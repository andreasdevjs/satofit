FAQ

How do I start earning Sats?

Download Satofit, create an account, and start walking. You'll earn Sats for every 4,000 steps.

Where is Satofit available?

Satofit is live in over 50 countries, and we're expanding rapidly!

How do I cash out my Sats?

Transfer your Sats to any Bitcoin wallet using the Lightning Network QR code in the app.

Is my data safe?

We use state-of-the-art encryption and never share your personal info.

How many Sats can I earn per day?

The amount varies, but on average, users can earn between 500-1000 Sats per day by reaching their step goals.

Do I need to have Bitcoin knowledge to use Satofit?

Not at all! Satofit is designed for both Bitcoin beginners and experts. We provide easy-to-understand information to help you learn as you earn.

Can I use Satofit with my fitness tracker?

Yes, Satofit integrates with popular fitness trackers and smartwatches to ensure accurate step counting.

Are there any fees for using Satofit?

Satofit is free to use. We make money through the ads you watch, allowing us to reward you with Sats without any fees.
